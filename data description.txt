All the original data files are stored in the folder data.
21-23hr.csv contains all the number of HR in every stadiums in MLB from 2021 to 2023 seasons. These data are collected from https://www.onlyhomers.com/daily.
ballparks.csv contains all the MLB stadiums information. In this file, all the distances are computed in feet. These data are from kaggle.
CIN_PIT.csv contains the HR per game hitted by Cincinnati Reds and Pittsburgh Pirates at their home stadium from 2021 to 2023.
The data were collected from www.teamrankings.com.
Both these two files' data are in correct form and types. And there is no missing value.
